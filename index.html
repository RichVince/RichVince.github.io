About Me

My advantage is simple: I’ve worked on the systems that security teams protect every day. That gives me practical context when looking at connectivity, misconfigurations, and security risk.

My background includes enterprise network implementation, firewall policy enforcement, technical support, VOIP/PBX systems, and real-world infrastructure troubleshooting across client-facing environments.

Through cybersecurity training and hands-on labs, I’ve continued to strengthen my skills in security monitoring, threat analysis, access control, vulnerability awareness, and defensive operations.

I’m currently targeting SOC Analyst and Junior Cybersecurity Analyst opportunities where I can contribute from day one with a strong infrastructure foundation, practical troubleshooting ability, and a defender’s mindset.
